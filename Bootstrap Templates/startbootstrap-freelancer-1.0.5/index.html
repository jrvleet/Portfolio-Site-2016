House of Fashion Brands

HOFB is a fashion marketplace web application that I collaborated with a team to create. My role was taking the design from the UX Artist and translating it into what you see when click on the link. I layed out all the elements using HTML5, CSS3, and Bootstrap inside an Express app using Handlebars and JSON. I also made the site fully responsive. The site is still in developement so I included images that will included in the full release of the app.

  • HTML5
  • CSS3
  • Responsive Design
  • Express

Oz Great and Powerful

Many scenes in Oz Great and Powerful needed to have digital tear burns applied to Mila Kunis's face to replace the practical makeup effects that were applied at the time of shooting. I did the motion tracking of her face in all these scenes and worked with the flame compositor to get it perfect in every shot. I also worked on two shots that had digital soldier munchkins marching down stairs. I animated the soldiers, fixed rigging issues and set up the cloth simulations on their cloaks.

  • Maya 3D
  • After Effects - Mocha
